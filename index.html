Commerce is
the catalyst.

Commerce has always been about the purposeful exchange of goods, services, and ideas to strengthen and advance communities. Here, we believe commerce can change the world. On Grounds, in Charlottesville, within our research centers, and through unparalleled educational experiences across the globe, we are inspiring students and empowering scholars to use commerce as a powerful catalyst for change.

McIntire Expansion Project

A transformational rethinking of our physical complex, with renovations to the interior and exterior spaces and the construction of a new building, Shumway Hall. The new complex will be a hub for Commerce students and pan-University collaboration, foster creativity and innovation, and inspire the next generation of business leaders.

View the Expansion Project

Small School. Global Footprint.

Get a world-renowned business education within the tight-knit learning community of a small school—while enjoying all of the benefits of attending a top-ranked public university.

Featured Program
M.S. in the Management of IT

The M.S. in the Management of IT from the University of Virginia's McIntire School of Commerce is a specialized, one-year graduate degree recognized for developing savvy technology managers and strategic decision-makers who excel at delivering business value through information technology.

  • One year, while you work
  • In-person sessions held monthly in Tysons, VA, combined with regular online learning
  • Build and strengthen your professional network with peers from your impressive cohort
  • Optional opportunity to participate in a global immersion experience in Mendoza, Argentina
  • Explore the M.S. in the Management of IT
